About ScaleSys

Innovating the future of precision measurement since 2005.

Our Mission

To empower industries with the most reliable and technologically advanced weighing solutions, ensuring accuracy, efficiency, and trust in every measurement. We are committed to continuous innovation and unparalleled customer support, driving progress for our partners worldwide.

Our Journey

From a small startup to a global leader in precision technology.

2005

Foundation of ScaleSys

ScaleSys was founded by a team of engineers with a passion for precision, aiming to revolutionize the weighing industry.

2010

First Major Innovation

Launched our first line of digital industrial scales, setting a new standard for durability and accuracy in harsh environments.

2015

Expansion into Software Solutions

Introduced ScaleSync, our proprietary software for seamless data integration and analysis, bridging the gap between hardware and data.

2023

Global Recognition

Awarded "Innovator of the Year" for our development of IoT-enabled smart scales, expanding our presence to over 50 countries.
